The useful details.

How the projects ran, what they delivered and the tools behind them.

What kinds of work do you typically take on?

Event research and curation, campaign creative, social and paid-test assets, CRM and email, partner materials, app testing, KPI reporting, community work and event operations.

What did you own at Pyxi?

I worked on Culture Edit across event research, curation, listings, campaign assets, CRM and email, paid tests, partner materials, app testing, guest coordination and feedback. In its first two months, it sold 70 tickets across 15+ London experiences and generated £1,000+ in revenue.

What did FilmPulse involve beyond the website?

Alongside managing FilmPulse online, I created content and presentation assets and helped deliver Kaleidoscope, a screening and networking event attended by 50+ people that established a connection with Screen Yorkshire.

Photoshop, Canva, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, HubSpot, CRM and email platforms, Excel, plus agentic AI tools for research, copy and production.
